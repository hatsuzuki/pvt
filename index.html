PVT test instructions

Thank you for participating in the tactical recharge trial. The intent of this test is to measure your level of alertness during your shift.

This test will run for 3 minutes. Please note that the test cannot be paused once it starts. Do give your full attention to the test.

We recommend temporarily turning on the Do Not Disturb function on your phone before starting the test so that you will not be distracted.

  1. The test will commence once you close this tutorial and press the green Start button.
  2. After you press Start, a red light will randomly appear every few seconds.
  1. Every time this light appears, tap or click on the dark part of the screen as fast as you can.
  2. If you are too slow, the light will disappear and be replaced by a "MISS". Wait for the light to appear again.
  3. However, when there is no light, DO NOT tap on the screen, otherwise this will be recorded as a false start ("FS").
  4. At the end of the test, you will be directed to a FormSG page to enter your results. Do not worry about keeping track of your score as the system will do it for you.

Once you are ready, close this tutorial and then click on the Start button.
